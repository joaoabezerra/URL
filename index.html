ABOUT

joao antonio bezerra, aKa NMLZE and fKa "NORMAL!ZE", is a dubstep music producer and DJ from recife, brazil. he is known for blending and combining the chaos of tearout, the horror of deathstep and the heaviness and agression of brostep and riddim. at 14 years old, he started his first connections with music, creating one of his first projects in FL studio and soundcloud. while he was 16 years old, he decided to be on musical hiatus, focusing on his high school works. at 18 years old, he will start a new era on his musical carrer, deciding to focus on heaviness, chaos, terror and agression on his sound design.
